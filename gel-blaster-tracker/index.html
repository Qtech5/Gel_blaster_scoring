Rules

Free-for-All (FFA)

⚙️ Setup

  • 5–8 players typical, but any number works
  • Each player starts with 2 lives

💀 Hits & Elimination

  • 1 hit = lose 1 life → −1 point
  • Lose a life → your kill streak resets to 0
  • Lose both lives → you are eliminated

🔥 Kill Streak Scoring

Points per kill are based on consecutive kills within your current life:

1st kill in a life+1 pt
2nd kill+2 pts
3rd kill+3 pts
4th kill+4 pts
…and so on

Dying resets your streak. Your next kill after a life loss is worth +1 again.

🔄 Trade Rule (Simultaneous Hit)

If two players hit each other at the same time:

  • Both players earn kill credit (at their next streak level)
  • Both take a hit: −1 life & −1 point each
  • Both streaks reset
Example: Player A is on their 3rd streak kill. Player B is on their 1st. They trade — A gets +3 and B gets +1. Then both get −1 life and −1 point. Net: A = +2, B = 0.

🏆 Win Bonuses (do not stack)

  • Last player alive: +3 points
  • Perfect Win — last alive with both lives intact: +8 points (not +3+8)

📊 Full Example

Player gets 3 kills in one life (+1 +2 +3 = +6), dies (−1), gets 2 more kills in second life (+1 +2 = +3), wins (+3 bonus).
Total: 6 − 1 + 3 + 3 = +11 pts
